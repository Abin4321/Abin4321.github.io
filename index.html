About Me

Hi! I'm Abin Bijoy, a final-year BTech student in Computer Science Engineering at Jyothi Engineering College, Thrissur. With a passion for technology and innovation, I have immersed myself in various aspects of software development, particularly web development and machine learning. My experience spans full-stack development using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) and deep learning techniques for artificial intelligence. Over the years, I've honed my skills by working on several real-world projects and completing internships in Full Stack Development and AI, provided by the ICT Academy Kerala.

I believe in the power of technology to solve complex problems and improve people's lives. My journey as a developer has been fueled by curiosity and a desire to build meaningful solutions. One of my key projects is a College Bus Management system, developed with CodeIgniter and PHP. This project demonstrates my commitment to creating safer digital environments. My goal is to continue exploring the possibilities in software engineering and AI by contributing to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.

I'm always eager to collaborate on exciting new projects and learn from others in the tech community. Feel free to connect with me to discuss potential opportunities, share ideas, or explore ways we can work together to bring impactful solutions to life.
